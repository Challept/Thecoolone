Kahoot Hack

Instructions

  1. Find the PIN of the Kahoot game you want to join and copy it to your clipboard.
  2. Paste the PIN into the appropriate field and press Continue.
  3. Enter any username you want and press Join Game.
  4. To get all the answers correct:
    • Scroll down to the section labeled Current Quiz Answer Correct.
    • Copy the quiz link from the top of the page and paste it into the provided field.
    • Set your desired accuracy (e.g., 95% correct).
  5. To add bots:
    • Scroll down, select Random Names, and press Add Bots.
    • Go back to Kahoot to see the bots in action!
